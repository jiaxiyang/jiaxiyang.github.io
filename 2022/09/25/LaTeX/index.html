    • O or E: to specify Odd or Even pages

    • L, C or R: for the Left, Centre and Right “zone” of the header or footer
  7. \pagestyle{fancyplain} doxygen refman.tex 删除之后页眉页脚不再有横杠
  8. pagestyle \pagestyle{⟨style⟩} link
    • empty: no headers or footers on pages
    • plain: no page headers, footers consist of a centered page number
    • headings: no footers, headers contains class-specific information and page number
    • myheadings: no footers, headers contains page number and user-supplied information

简历模板

  1. The CV presents a full history of your academic credentials, so the length of the document is variable. In contrast, a resume presents a concise picture of your skills and qualifications for a specific position, so length tends to be shorter and dictated by years of experience (generally 1-2 pages).

  2. CV 是履历，Resume 是简历。

    • Resume，简述于求职相关的教育背景和工作经历，其目的在于说服用人单位雇用自己；
    • CV，Curriculum Vitae 事集中说明学术工作，不重视与文化程度和学习成绩无直接关系的资料。
